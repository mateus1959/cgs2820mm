Life at Honeymoon State Park

Resting Shorebird Haven
Black skimmers, American oystercatchers, plovers and terns spend time feeding and nesting here.
Turtle Nesting
Both Loggerheads and green sea turtles nest on Honeymoon Island.
Osprey Trail
Hike along a slash pine forest where some trees are 200 years old. You may see bald eagles, osprey, and great horned owls.
Reptiles
A large population of gopher tortoises inhabit the island as well as several species of snakes including the Eastern diamondback rattlesnake.
